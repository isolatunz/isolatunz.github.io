Mental Health

Understanding Anxiety

Anxiety is a natural feeling that everyone experiences at times in life. It can become overwhelming when it starts affecting daily activities, school, sleep or relationships.

Common Signs of Anxiety

  • Constant worrying
  • Feeling nervous, on edge or easily fustrated
  • Trouble concentrating
  • Difficulty sleeping
  • Avoiding situations because of fear

Ways to Cope

  • Practice deep breathing
  • Get enough sleep
  • Staying physically active
  • Talk to trusted friends, family members or adults
  • Take breaks when feeling overwhelmed

Need More Support?

If anxiety is making it difficult to enjoy life, focus in school or take care of yourself, consider reaching out to a trusted adult, school counselor, or mental health professional.
