About NextStepNYC

NextStepNYC was created to introduce opportunities, health resources and health information to more teens. In the website there we will add information about opportunities that available around Nyc and share wellness tips for teens. Our goal is to make it easier for young people to get educated on mental health, wellness, and also help teens find opportunities that leads them to their future career path. Whether you’re looking for a summer program, ways to improve your health, or more opportunities for experience, NextStepNYC is here to help.

Why I Started It

As a high school student, I know it can be hard to find opportunities and resources easily, Therefore, I created NextStepNYC to make this process easier and to share information to help students succeed and know they’re not alone.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help students find the resources, programs, and opportunities they need to grow, learn, and succeed.
