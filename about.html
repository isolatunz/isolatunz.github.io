About Us

About NextStepNYC

NextStepNYC was created to help teens find opportunities, resources and wellness information. In this website we will share information about opportunities available around NYC to teens and share wellness tips. Our goal is to make it easier for teens to learn about mental health, wellness and gain opportunities that'll prep them for their future and support them. Whether you’re looking for a program, ways to improve your health, or to learn ways to help yourself and those around you, NextStepNYC is here to help.

Why I Started It

As a high school student, I know it can be hard to find opportunities and resources easily. Therefore, I created NextStepNYC to make this process easier and to share information to help students succeed and know they’re not alone.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help students find the resources, programs, and opportunities they need to grow, learn, and succeed.
